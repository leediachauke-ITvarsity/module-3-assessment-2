BANG BANG SHRIMP

Price: R68

Serving size: Medium

About the meal

It’s tender shrimp coated in crispy breadcrumbs and a sweet and spicy, creamy sauce with just the right amount of chili heat. That’s where it gets the name “Bang Bang.”

The sweet component to the sauce and those little red pepper flakes come from sweet Thai chili sauce, like the kind many places serve with their wontons and egg rolls.

Extras

  1. Sweet chill sauce
  2. Hot sauce
  3. Honey
